Take a minute for youself, with us

The green Bar Oasi is located on the main summer walk of Bolsena, our guests love to enjoy an icecream while visiting the lake front. We focused first on the method to give the highest quality and most hot-resistant product possible for an ice-cream, we obtained satisfying results according to us and our guests.

Since our opening in 2008, we searched for new ways to evolve our iceream culture, after attending different national events, in 2012 we found our first teacher, Donata Panciera, globally known for her expertise, and respectful way to choose and treat ingredients. That experience changed exponencially the way we think of a new icecream flavour.

In 2015 we were ready to renew our working style and found a perfect match in Sergio Colalucci, an italian champion of icecream making. With him we gave a more modern touch to our process, we restyled the serving our glass-cups, we created cakes and different icecream with a crunchy layer outside and a soft artisan icecream inside. The peak of our work with Sergio has been reached in 2019, we created a salted icecream served with apple chips, a salted biscuit outside, and Coregone inside, a local fish of our lake. Our work characteristics have been deeply influenced by the encounter with Sergio Colalucci.
